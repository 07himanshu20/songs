ABOUT THE MOVIE

Running against the clock, IAF squadron leader VIJAY KARNIK created history with his determination and his love for his motherland. A daredevil story of guts and glory. The film is based on the true incident, On December 8, 1971, Pakistani Sabre jets dropped 14 Napalm bombs on the Air Force base in Bhuj. The attack damaged the airstrip, halting all flight operations.
